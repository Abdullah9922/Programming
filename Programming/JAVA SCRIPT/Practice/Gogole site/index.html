About Me

Turning Ideas into Digital Reality

I'm Ishtyaq Ahmed Abdullah, a Computer Science & Engineering student at Daffodil International University. My journey in tech started with a simple curiosity: how does software actually work? That question has since grown into a deep passion for building things that matter.

I specialize in crafting clean, efficient, and user-centric applications — from interactive web experiences to small games and utility tools. I believe great software is invisible: it just works, and it works beautifully.

My long-term goal is to evolve into a professional full-stack engineer who contributes to meaningful products. I'm especially drawn to the intersection of AI and web technology, where I see the most exciting problems waiting to be solved.

When I'm not coding, I'm exploring new algorithms, tinkering with game mechanics, or simply staying curious about what's next in the world of technology.
